Maze Map Editor

Draw a 32×32 maze — each cell = 4×4 pixels on the CC3200 OLED.

How to play

Runner (Player 1 — white)
Navigate the maze using the accelerometer and reach the green goal tile before the Hunter catches you. You win by escaping.
Hunter (Player 2 — red)
Chase the Runner through the maze. Press SW2 when you are close enough to attack. Catch the Runner before they reach the goal to win.
512px canvas → 32×32 grid → 128×128 on OLED
